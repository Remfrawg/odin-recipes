There's Vomit on His Sweater Already (see below)

Debbie Nelson's Famous Italian Pasta

This dish was made famous in a performance by Marshall Mathers of his hit song Lose Yourself (ft. Sweater). Using this recipe you can relive that tear jerking, knee crumbling moment in time.

Ingredients

  • Questionable parenting
  • Sweater
  • 2 Knees or knee substitutes, preferrably weak
  • A calm demeanor
  • Short term memory loss triggered by high pressure situations
  • Early 2000s nostalgia
  • Pre-made spaghetti
  • Stopwatch

Secondary Ingredients

  • Bars
  • Hydrogen peroxide (H202)

Directions

  1. Set stopwatch for 2 hours
  2. Watch 8-Mile
  3. Remove sweater
  4. Bleach hair using hydrogen peroxide
  5. Continually fail at rap battling
  6. Time this correctly so that the time runs out on your stopwatch now
  7. Lose Yourself
  8. Win rap battle
  9. Enjoy!
