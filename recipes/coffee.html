Coffee

America's Second Favorite Pastime

Caffiene addiction isn't a joke, but Starbucks coffee is. here at TOP we'll teach you how to save money and make a darn good cup of coffee.

Coffee Ingredients

  • Coffee grounds or similar(dirt,chocolate cake, etc.)
  • Beer or beer substitute
  • Lawyer or video camera

Cup Ingredients

  • Cup
  • Back up cup

Directions

  1. Brief lawyer or camera on the dangers of coffee
  2. Bring beer or substitute to 213 degrees Fahrenheit (F)
  3. Pour coffee grounds or substitute into boiling liquid
  4. Let simmer for 3-4.5 hours for maximum absorbtion
  5. Bring coffee down to 33 degrees (F)
  6. Dip cup in the pot, try to avoid drinking directly from the pot
  7. Enjoy
